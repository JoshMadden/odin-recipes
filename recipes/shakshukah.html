Shakshukah

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Tomatoes
  • Pepperoni's
  • Spices
  • Butter
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cut up peps and heat in pan till oil'd up
  2. Add Tomatoes and cover, cook to wilt
  3. Mash to paste and add Spices
  4. Cook till reduced then add Butter
  5. once mixed, make holes for eggs
  6. crack in Eggs, sprinkle salt all over
  7. Cover and let cook till Eggs are done
